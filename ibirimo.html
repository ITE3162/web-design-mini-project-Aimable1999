AMATEKA N'UMUCO BY'ABANYARWANDA
================================

Mumateka n’umuco w’abanyarwanda, tugiramo bimwe mu byaranze abanyarwanda ndetse

ndetse tugira n’igice twahariye gusabana kubintu biranga igihugu cy’u Rwanda.
